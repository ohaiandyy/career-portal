❓ How to Use Career Hub ✕
🔐 Signing In
👤
Select your name on the login screen, enter your 4-digit PIN, and hit Sign In. Each person only sees their own jobs and profile — no one else's data is visible.
🔑
Forgot your PIN? Go to Settings → Change My PIN to update it yourself. Or ask your Admin to reset it.
⚙️
Admin login (PIN: ask your administrator) — sees all candidates, edits profiles, manages users, and exports data.
🔍 Finding Jobs
🔍
Search for Jobs — click this in the sidebar and wait ~15 seconds. The AI searches live job boards and returns ~20 real openings tailored to your profile. A 30-second cooldown prevents accidental double-searches.
➕
Load 10 More Jobs — appears at the bottom of your list after the first search. Runs a fresh AI search focused on different companies and industries you haven't seen yet.
🔗
Add a Job You Found — paste any job URL (LinkedIn, Indeed, company site) and click "Fetch Details" to auto-fill the form. Or fill it in manually.
🔎
Filters at the top narrow results by Remote, Hybrid, Travel, Starred, or Applied. Click any filter to activate — click All to reset.
📋 Managing Jobs
⭐
Star a job to mark it as a priority. Starred jobs are highlighted gold and appear under the ⭐ Starred filter.
✅
Mark Applied after you send your resume. The card turns green and appears under ✅ Applied so you can track where you've applied.
📋
Track Progress — use the dropdown on each card to move a job through stages: Researching → Applied → Interview → Offer. Click "Job Tracker" at the top right to see your full pipeline as a board.
✕
Dismiss a job to remove it from your list. A 5-second undo button appears immediately after — or restore dismissed jobs any time by clicking "🗑 Dismissed" in your sidebar stats.
▾
Details expands a card to show qualifications, benefits, and a private notes field. Notes are saved automatically and only visible to you and Admin.
✏️ Your Profile
✏️
Edit your bio — click directly on your bio text to edit it. Your bio is used by the AI when searching — keep it current and specific for better results.
🏷️
Skills & Keywords — type a skill and press Enter to add it. Click × to remove. More accurate skills = better AI job matches. Use "✨ Suggest More Skills" to let AI recommend additions.
🎯
Search Targets — the most important field. Tells the AI exactly what roles and industries to find. Click "✏️ Edit" to customize, or use "✨ Help me write this" to have AI generate it from your profile. Be specific — "HR Director / CHRO, VP Operations — tech, finance, consulting" works better than "HR jobs."
📄
Update Profile from Resume — paste your resume text and AI will extract your skills and rewrite your bio. Shows a before/after preview so you can approve changes. Includes a 10-second undo after applying.
⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
⌨️
S — Search for jobs  ·  F — Toggle filters  ·  A — Add a job  ·  T — Toggle tracker view
1 All  ·  2 Remote  ·  3 Starred  ·  4 Applied  ·  5 To Review
Press ? anytime to see a quick reminder. Shortcuts are disabled when typing in a field.
💾 Saving & Data
💾
Auto-save — everything saves automatically. The "✓ Saved" indicator in the header confirms each save. No manual save button needed.
⚡
Data lives in your browser — jobs, notes, and profile are stored locally. Connect Airtable in Admin Settings to sync across devices.
📊
Export CSV (Admin only) — downloads all jobs across all candidates as a spreadsheet for Excel or Google Sheets.
