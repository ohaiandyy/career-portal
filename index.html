❓ How to Use Career Hub ✕
🔐 Signing In
👤
Select your name on the login screen, enter your 4-digit PIN, and hit Sign In. Each person only sees their own jobs — no one else's.
⚙️
Admin login can see all three candidates, edit everyone's profiles, change PINs, and export data. Use Settings → Change PINs to update any PIN.
🔍 Finding Jobs
🔍
Search for New Jobs — click this button and wait 10–20 seconds. The AI searches live job boards and returns 10–15 real current openings tailored to your profile. Each search may return different results, so you can run it multiple times to build up your list.
➕
Add a Job You Found — paste any job URL (LinkedIn, Indeed, company site) and click "Fetch Details" to auto-fill the form. Or fill it in manually. Great for jobs you spot yourself anywhere online.
🔎
Filters at the top let you narrow down by Remote, Hybrid, Travel, New (added this session), Starred, or Applied. Click any filter — click again or click "All" to reset.
📋 Managing Jobs
⭐
Star a job to save it as a priority. Starred jobs are highlighted in gold and show up under the ⭐ Starred filter. Use this for roles you definitely want to apply to.
✅
Mark Applied after you send in your resume. The card turns green and it shows in the ✅ Applied filter so you can track where you've applied.
✕
Dismiss a job you're not interested in — it slides out of the list. Dismissed jobs don't come back unless you clear and re-search.
▾
Details button on each card expands to show qualifications, benefits, and a private notes field. Notes are only visible to you (and Admin).
✏️ Editing Your Profile
✏️
Edit your bio by clicking the pencil icon next to your name. Click outside the text box to save. Your bio is used by the AI when searching for jobs — keeping it accurate helps find better matches.
🏷️
Skills & Keywords — type a skill and press Enter (or click +) to add it. Click × on any skill to remove it. The more accurate your skills list, the better the AI job matches.
🎯
Search Targets — the text below your skills tells the AI what roles and industries to look for. Click "Edit search targets" to customize it. You can be as broad or specific as you like.
📄 Resume Import
📄
Import Resume — paste your resume text and the AI will extract your skills and rewrite your profile summary automatically. This is a great way to get your profile set up quickly. You can edit anything it generates.
💾 Saving & Syncing
💾
Auto-save — everything saves automatically to your browser. You don't need to click save anywhere.
⚡
Airtable sync (optional) — if Admin has connected Airtable in Settings, your jobs automatically back up to the cloud so they're accessible on any device. If not connected, data stays in this browser only.
📊
Export CSV (Admin only) — downloads all jobs across all three candidates as a spreadsheet you can open in Excel or Google Sheets.
